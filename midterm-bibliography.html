My Master's CAP

In my Master's degree Comphrehensive Assessment Assignment (thesis alternative), I explored the use of video games in the English classroom and created a sample lesson plan for teachers to utilize. My CAP focused specifically on the 2011 title Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim and the use of folklore-like elements in video games to create more lived-in, dynamic, and immersive fictional worlds. I provide teachers with readings, explanations of how to access games, and many other tools they may need.

View and/or download a PDF of my CAP via Google Drive.

The Works Cited in my CAP lists many additional resources related to this subject!
